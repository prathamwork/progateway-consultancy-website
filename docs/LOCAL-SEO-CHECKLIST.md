# Local SEO, Google Business Profile and Citation Checklist

## Google Business Profile

- [ ] Use the exact real-world name: **Progateway Consultancy**. Do not append keywords unless they are part of the registered and displayed business name.
- [ ] Confirm the verified address matches: **U-20, Sagar Complex, Eru Char Rasta, Navsari, Gujarat 396450**.
- [ ] Confirm phone, website, email and hours match the website exactly.
- [ ] Select the closest genuinely applicable primary category in the live GBP interface; add only relevant secondary categories.
- [ ] Add every real service offered, using natural service names and accurate descriptions.
- [ ] Link the website with a measured URL, for example: `https://www.progatewayconsultancy.com/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp_navsari`.
- [ ] Use `/contact` as the appointment/enquiry destination where the profile supports it.
- [ ] Add real exterior, entrance, reception, consultant and working-process photographs.
- [ ] Publish useful posts about document checklists, compliance reminders and new resources.
- [ ] Add factual questions and answers; never seed misleading claims.
- [ ] Check for duplicate or practitioner listings and request correction/removal where necessary.
- [ ] Review business hours before holidays and temporary closures.
- [ ] Keep the physical address only for Navsari. Do not create fake offices in Surat, Ahmedabad or Vadodara.

## Honest review collection

Use the direct “Ask for reviews” URL from the verified Google Business Profile. Replace `SITE.googleReviewUrl` when that direct URL is available.

Suggested neutral WhatsApp request:

> Hello [Client Name], thank you for working with Progateway Consultancy. Your honest feedback helps us improve and helps other customers understand our service. You may share your experience here: [review link]. Please mention what service you used and what was helpful. There is no obligation to leave a review.

Rules:

- Ask all eligible clients consistently, not only happy clients.
- Do not offer discounts, gifts or benefits for reviews.
- Do not ask for a five-star or positive review.
- Do not prevent dissatisfied clients from reaching the review link.
- Respond professionally to every review without disclosing private financial or identity information.

## Citation consistency

For every listing, use the same:

- Business name
- Navsari address
- Primary phone number
- Website canonical URL
- Business hours
- Short factual description
- Correct categories

Priority profile types:

- [ ] Google Business Profile
- [ ] Bing Places
- [ ] Apple Business Connect
- [ ] Relevant Navsari or Gujarat chamber/association directory
- [ ] Trusted Indian business directories where the listing can be claimed and maintained
- [ ] Genuine partner or supplier directories
- [ ] Relevant professional or certification-body partner listings, only where authorised

Avoid:

- Mass-submission packages
- Hundreds of low-quality directory listings
- Fake locations or virtual-office listings
- Duplicate profiles with keyword variations
- Inconsistent phone numbers or addresses

## Local authority outreach

- Offer a practical GST, registration or documentation workshop to a local business association.
- Contribute a fact-checked checklist to a local publication or entrepreneurship group.
- Ask genuine professional partners to link to the relevant service/resource page when useful to their audience.
- Sponsor or support a real local business event only when there is a genuine relationship and disclosure.
- Publish original resources worthy of citation instead of exchanging exact-match links.
- Build relationships with accountants, advocates, company secretaries, banks, insurers and certification professionals while accurately describing each party’s role.
